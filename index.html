Make It Work, Sis ✨

Just bought a wig and it’s giving… “meh”? Don’t toss it. Here’s how to finesse it — because every crown can shine with the right care.

  • Steam It: Use a handheld steamer (or hang it in a steamy bathroom) to soften fibers and add natural volume.
  • Pluck the Part: A little tweezer action at the parting makes it look way more realistic.
  • Hot Water Dip: (Synthetic wigs only) Dip in hot water for a smoother look — especially after curls get tangled.
  • Thin Out the Density: Use thinning shears to reduce bulk if it’s looking too “wiggy.”
  • Add Baby Hairs: Lay some edges with gel for a softer, custom hairline.
  • Cut it Your Way: Don’t be afraid to trim or layer it. Even a $20 wig can serve if the shape is right!
  • Dry Shampoo Trick: Too shiny? Hit it with a little dry shampoo to tone it down.

Remember: it’s not just the wig — it’s the confidence you wear it with. We got you.
